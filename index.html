New Install and Fab Shop and New Employee at Mule

Mule Expedition Outfitters is excited to announce that Jon Ament (formerly of JA Custom Works) has joined our company as part owner and full-time fabricator!

Jon brings with him a wealth of fabrication skills and motorsports experience. He has worked on jets in the US Navy; has a long history with high performance cars; and has managed a high end race shop and its international road racing program, including the factory built Mustang Challenge Series and Grand Am Continental Series.

For the last 2.5 years, Jon has owned his own fabrication shop building everything from Cafe motorcycles and expedition vans and trailers to restoring vintage cars. He is personable, honest, professional and highly skilled. Joe and I are stoked to have him on board! Please join us in welcoming him next time you see him.

Find Us Here!

This development also brings the addition of our new, state-of-the art custom work and install shop at #60 NW Gilman Blvd (right across the street from our retail showroom in Issaquah). This three vehicle shop enables us to provide professional installation and custom design and fabrication work at a fantastic, easily accessible location. Jon will be at this location part-time through November and full-time beginning Dec 2nd.

Hours will be the same as our retail showroom at 98 NE Gilman Blvd (same building as the XXX Root Beer Drive In in Issaquah): 10am-6pm, Tue-Sat. Installs and custom work can be scheduled through the retail store.
